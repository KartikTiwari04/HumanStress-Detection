About StressSense AI

This system detects stress levels by analyzing your interaction patterns:

  • Typing Speed: Variations in typing rhythm and speed
  • Mouse Randomness: Irregular mouse movements
  • Click Patterns: Frequency and intensity of clicks
  • Error Rate: Backspaces and corrections

All data is processed locally and anonymously. No personal data is stored.
